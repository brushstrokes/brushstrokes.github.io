How your overall grade is calculated

Your overall grade is (homework average)*.2 + (curved midterm)*.4 + (curved final)*.4
This grade will be converted to a letter grade as follows: 
A+  ≥ 98      B+ 87-89      C+ 77-79      D+ 67-69
A  93-97      B  83-86      C  73-76      D 60-66
A- 90-92      B- 80-82      C- 70-72      F 0-59
Curves:
(curved midterm) = grade^0.65 * 102^0.35
(the curve is only applied to grades lower than 102) 
A+, A, or A- (28)
B+, B, or B- (13)
C+, C, or C- (17)
D+, D, or F (10)

(curved final) = ? 




Each curve will be decided after the exam is graded.
