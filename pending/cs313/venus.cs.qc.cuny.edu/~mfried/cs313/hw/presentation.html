Presentation (extra credit)

If you do a good job on the presentation, it will increase your average grade by up to 3 points. You will work in groups of 3 students.

Email me your topic (chosen from the list below) and the names of your group members.
Each group must pick a different topic, so wait for my approval before you begin working.

What to talk about in the presentation

Your presentation should last 15-20 minutes. You should prepare slides and send them to me at least 1 day in advance. If you don't have time to explain everything fully, focus on the most important points. Be prepared to answer questions.

If you pick a data structure topic, you should:
  • Explain why the data structure is useful (give an example of a situation where you would use it).
  • Explain visually how it's implemented, e.g. how to search or add an element.
  • Explain the runtimes. If there are any probabilities involved, then explain those as well.
If you pick an algorithm topic, you should:
  • Explain the problem that the algorithm solves.
  • Explain the algorithm. Show a sample problem instance and how to use the algorithm to solve it.
  • Depending on the algorithm, you may want to discuss runtimes and/or compare the algorithm to other algorithms.

References

You must use and cite at least 2 reliable references, for example textbooks, research papers, or video lectures from universities. Let me know if you need help finding references.
