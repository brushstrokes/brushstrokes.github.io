A Short Story About Last Evening

Author: Gilbert R. Salas

"Lets get out of this dump" Tom said whilst walking up to me.

"Okay, that's probably a good idea" I responed.

We walk down to my car without looking back. Tonight is the night my band, Examine, is playing their 3rd show. It's a long ride -- but it's definitely way too late to cancel. I know that my classes start tommorow morning at eight in the morning. So I quickly convince myself that my band will jam, then I could split as soon as possible. I lock the key inside the ignition as I climb in and start the car up immediately. It's cold, lets get this engine warm before my hands stiffen up. Soon enough the heat begins to radiate and we're off.
