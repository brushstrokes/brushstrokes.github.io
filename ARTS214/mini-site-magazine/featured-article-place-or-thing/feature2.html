ARTS 214 - Featured Place Page

July 10 - 14, 2016

CiscoLive! is a computer networking convention held annually at various locations. This year the conference is being held in Las Vegas, Nevada - and next year it will be held in Berlin. Professional get the opportunity to network amongst one another socially all while researching and premiering state of the art networking technology. Cisco System charges a whopping $2495 for a full conference pass, but you're still able to get into the convention for $69. This gives you the status of 'explorer' (sounds obscure to me too).
