A Short Story About Last Evening

Page 2


We'd finally just reached exit 49 on the Souther State Parkway. "Dang man this place is a trip, I've been driving for an hour already!" Weaving in and out of traffic. Fast forward and we're approaching the venue. Green light, clear of traffic, let me just make a u-turn. A sudden loud screech causes my head to swing to the left as a fast approaching BMW convertible manages to come to a stop only a slight movement away from my passenger side door.

I could hazily see an older man screaming curses at me. "You need to move back about a foot!" I yell He reluctanly shifts his car into reverse and moves. Cursing him and myself, I punch the gas and finish my turn.

"Jesus, count your blessings." I say going to grab hold of the white rosary beads hanging my rear-view mirror.
